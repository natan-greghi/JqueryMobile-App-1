Zagara

Broodmother of the Swarm

Tipo: Especialista

Hist√≥ria :

Like many of the Queen of Blades' creations, the Broodmother Zagara was birthed of the terrible ash and heat of Char. Unlike most Zerg, Broodmothers are capable of complex problem-solving and multi-tasking, and are possessed of the ability to command members of their race with fewer sapient traits. Each Broodmother is an important piece of the greater Zerg organism, able to provide large groups of Zerg with a nearly "independent" perspective.

  • Habilidades

Habilidades

  • D: Creep Tumor

    Cooldown: 15s

    Lay a Creep Tumor that generates Creep. You and your summons move 20% faster on Creep and restore 1.8125 (+0.3125 per level) Health a second. Tumors last 240 seconds.

  • Q: Baneling Barrage

    Cooldown: 10s

    Launch 4 Banelings that deal 40 (+5 per level) splash damage each.

  • W: Hunter Killer

    Cooldown: 14s

    Summon a Hydralisk to attack a single target, dealing 46 (+6 per level) damage per second. Lasts 8 seconds.

  • E: Infested Drop

    Cooldown: 12s

    Bombard target area with a Zerg Drop Pod for 72 (+12 per level) damage. The pod spawns 2 Roachlings that deal 15 (+2 per level) damage per second and last for 8 seconds.

  • R: Devouring Maw

    Cooldown: 100s

    Summon a Devouring Maw that deals 99 (+24 per level) damage and devours enemies for 4 seconds. Devoured enemies cannot fight and take 40 (+2 per level) damage per second.

  • R: Nydus Network

    Cooldown: 60s

    Summon a Nydus Worm at target location that you can enter by right-clicking. While inside, you exit by targeting a Nydus Worm with R or right-clicking near the Worm. Maximum 4 Nydus Worms.
