Tyrande

High Priestess of Elune

Tipo: Suporte

História :

Tyrande is the passionate, compassionate, brave, wise and occasionally rash, leader of the Night Elf (Kaldorei) people, with beauty almost beyond compare. As such she is also the former leader of the Night Elf Sentinels. She is also the High Priestess of Elune, chosen by Elune herself, and worships Elune with total submission and adoration. She loves Malfurion Stormrage and will do almost anything to help or protect him. Tyrande is a caring soul at her core, and is willing to do anything to protect those she loves and is sworn to protect — which these days means the entire Kaldorei race.

  • Habilidades

Habilidades

  • D: Hunter's Mark

    Cooldown: 20s

    Marks a target enemy, increasing all damage taken by 25% and revealing them for 4 seconds. Cannot be used on Structures.

  • Q: Light of Elune

    Cooldown: 8s

    Heal yourself for 74 (+14 per level) . If cast on an ally, also heals them for 148 (+28 per level) Health.

  • W: Sentinel

    Cooldown: 18s

    Send an Owl that travels across the battleground revealing its path. Deals 76 (+16 per level) damage to the first enemy Hero hit and reveals them for 5 seconds.

  • E: Lunar Flare

    Cooldown: 12s

    After a short delay, deal 108 (+18 per level) damage and stun enemies in the target area for 1 second.

  • R: Shadowstalk

    Cooldown: 70s

    Stealth you and all allied Heroes for 8 seconds, and reveal all enemy Heroes for 3 seconds.

  • R: Starfall

    Cooldown: 100s

    Deal 26 (+6 per level) damage per second and slow enemies by 20% in an area. Lasts 8 seconds.
