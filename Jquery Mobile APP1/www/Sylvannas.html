Habilidades

  • D: Black Arrows

    Basic Attacks and Abilities stun Minions, Mercenaries, and Towers for 1 second.

  • Q: Withering Fire

    Cooldown: 2s

    Shoot the closest enemy for 28 (+3 per level) damage, preferring Heroes. Stores 5 charges. Gain 1 charge on nearby enemy Minion or Mercenary deaths, and 3 charges on nearby enemy Hero deaths.

  • W: Shadow Dagger

    Cooldown: 10s

    Deals 13.5 (+3.5 per level) damage and an additional 54 (+14 per level) damage over 2 seconds to target unit. The effect spreads to nearby targets.

  • E: Haunting Wave

    Cooldown: 11s

    Send forth a wave of banshees dealing 41 (+11 per level) damage to all targets. Reactivate to teleport to the banshees' location.

  • R: Possession

    Cooldown: 12s

    Force an enemy Minion to fight for you. It gains 20% Attack Damage. Costs 5 charges to convert a Catapult Minion.

  • R: Wailing Arrow

    Cooldown: 90s

    Shoot an arrow that can be reactivated to deal 158 (+18 per level) damage and Silencing enemies in an area making them unable to use Abilities for 2.5 seconds. The arrow detonates automatically when it reaches maximum range.
